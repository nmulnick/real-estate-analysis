2. Net Present Value (NPV) ?NPV converts all future cash flows to today's dollars using the discount rate. A higher NPV means more value in present terms. Scenario A's NPV is simply today's after-tax proceeds.

3. Internal Rate of Return (IRR) ?IRR is the annualized return of choosing to hold instead of sell. It treats the Scenario A proceeds as an opportunity cost (negative cash flow at Year 0). If IRR exceeds your reinvestment rate, holding is the better financial decision.
